General Application Process

Counseling and Guidance Sessions

In the counseling sessions, students will be informed about the programs most appropriate to their needs. A counseling session will help you understand what and where you would like to study while acknowledging previous education, interests, career outcomes, and budgets. Students will be informed on the necessary documents like academics, Language proficiency tests, translations & verification of documentation.

Plan out the cost. Students will be informed well on the cost of education and additional costs necessary like living, travel, and stationary expenses while studying abroad. You can carefully study your estimated cost to study abroad.

Test preparation class

If you do not have the English language proficiency requirements such as IELTS, TOEFL, or PTE we will provide you with the best preparation classes. Once you sign up for our class, we will register and book the test dates for you as well.

Application

Application Deadlines, documents, Academic test scores requirements, and Language proficiency test scores, vary according to the course, education provider, and country you are applying to. We will send your application to the educational provider of your choice once you submit the necessary documents.
When the college finds you an eligible applicant, they will provide an offer explaining the details and description of the course; course fee; orientation date; course commencement date & course completion date; campus location; total tuition fee; and initial tuition fee required for payment in advance.

Enrollment

You are qualified for enrollment after you receive the offer letter. Providing financial proof is the major part of enrollment. You will be informed of all the documents and evidence necessary for the Financial Part to show and verify your education funds. You will provide the financial documents and pay the required initial fee to the college.

Confirmation of Enrollment

You will receive confirmation of enrollment when the education provider accepts your financial documents and receives your fee payment.

Medical Test Report & Health Insurance

Another compulsory requirement is to provide a medical report of physical examination and Health insurance. A medical report is a basic requirement for which we will assist you with information on cost, booking the medical test date, and submitting the report.
Health insurance coverage helps students cover the cost of medical treatment, doctor’s visits, prescriptions, and other health-related expenses while studying abroad. Health insurance coverage also differs in cost and coverage depending on the country you are applying to. (*note-Depending on the country and education provider, you might have to get the Health insurance upfront while applying for enrollment)

Visa Requirement & Visa lodgment

You will be informed of additional documents if necessary. Once you provide complete documents, all verified documents will be processed for your visa application. The processing time of the application can vary and is not constant.

Visa Grant

Once the visa officer approves your eligibility, you will receive a “visa grant”. The validity of the visa depends on the course duration. You will be informed of the details of the length of stay duration by the letter.

Accommodation & Travel Arrangements and Pre-Departure Briefing

After you receive a visa grant letter, our counselors will help you choose and book the best accommodation on campus, homestay, or private rental if you haven’t already decided on your accommodation. We will also assist help you with booking flight tickets and currency exchange.

Pre-Departure Information

KTM Consulting provides a pre-departure briefing class to build your confidence after landing in a foreign land. We will prepare and inform you of all the necessary documents and belongings you take with you. You will learn about students’ lifestyles abroad, local culture, places and attractions near your campus, banking and foreign exchange, and advice on work and internships. The Pre-Departure Briefing class will allow you to meet other applicants who might be attending your education provider.
